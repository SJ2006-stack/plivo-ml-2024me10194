1. Problem & metric

At each pause: is the user done (EOT) or mid-thought (hold)? Official score = mean response delay (ms) at ≤5% interrupted turns (lower better). Hidden test is mostly Hindi. No pretrained ASR/VAD.

2. Solution

Causality

Features use only x[:pause_start]. Never pause duration / post-pause audio (also removed from sample weights after a leak was found).

Signal

  • F0 via librosa.piptrack (fmin=50; pyin abandoned after HI OOF regression)
  • Energy decay, final lengthening, relative final pitch (fall/rise), spectral shape, IPI / pause_index
  • First-pause gates: soften rise penalty; cut fall boost when pause_index==0

Model & inference

Unified LR + shallow HGB on full EN+HI → starter/models/unified.joblib. predict.py --data_dir <folder> --out predictions.csv loads the saved model (no refit) and works on never-seen folders with the same labels schema. Columns: turn_id,pause_index,p_eot.

English

Silence waits 1600 ms. Our best handout ship answers at 1000 ms with usable ranking from causal prosody — hundreds of ms sooner inside the 5% interruption budget.

Silence is 850 ms. Handout ship reaches 783 ms (AUC 0.86). Honest OOF best is ~840 — still the generalization target. We beat status quo on English delay and Hindi handout delay/ranking; honest Hindi freeze remains the open gap.
